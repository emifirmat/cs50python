It is played with a Spanish deck excluding 8 (eights), 9 (nines) and jokers. Total cards: 40
Cards hierarchy from higher to lower value is:
1 sword
1 club
7 swords
7 coins
3 all
2 all
1 coin and cup
12 all
11 all
10 all
7 cups and clubs
6 all
5 all
4 all
E.g: 7 coins is higher than any 2, but lower than 7 swords. 2 coins is equal to any other 2.  
10, 11 and 12 are known as "the oldies".
