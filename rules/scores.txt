The goal of the game is to reach 15 or 30 points, which is decided before starting playing.
In each row, you can earn game points from envido and from truco.
Possible score points to earn in a row:
- Minimum from envido: 0 (None called it)
- Maximum from envido: Left-to-win (The player with less score points lost falta envido)
- Minimum from truco: 1 (Not called or rejected)
- Maximum from truco: 4 (Vale 4 was accepted)
- Minimum in a row: 1
- Maximum in a row: Left-to-win
