“Truco” is a card game very popular in Argentina. 
It is played with a Spanish deck and the goal is to reach a specific score that in this game can be 15 or 30. 
This version only supports 1v1 game and excludes “flor” rules.
If you want to know more about the full version card game, please visit: https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Truco_argentino
